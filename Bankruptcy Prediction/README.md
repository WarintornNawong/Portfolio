# COMPANY BANKRUPTCY PREDICTION
## INTRODUCTION
### DATASET
The data I utilized to train model were derived from Kaggle Dataset in [Company Bankruptcy Prediction](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/fedesoriano/company-bankruptcy-prediction). The data were collected from the Taiwan Economic Journal for the years 1999 to 2009. Company bankruptcy was defined based on the business regulations of the Taiwan Stock Exchange with Source from Deron Liang and Chih-Fong Tsai, National Central University, Taiwan.
